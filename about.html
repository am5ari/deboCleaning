Meet The Owner

Darius Jones-Gibson

Founder of Debos Cleaning

Darius Jones-Gibson graduated from Samford University in 2016, where he played collegiate basketball before pursuing his dream of playing professionally overseas for four years. His experience as a professional athlete instilled in him the values of discipline, resilience, leadership, and a relentless commitment to excellence. Following his basketball career, Darius transitioned into entrepreneurship by founding Debo’s Cleaning Service. What began as a vision has grown into a trusted commercial cleaning company known for delivering dependable, high-quality janitorial and specialty cleaning services. Under his leadership, Debo’s Cleaning Service proudly serves commercial facilities, schools, medical offices, retail spaces, construction projects, and residential clients with a commitment to professionalism, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction. Darius has built the company on a foundation of integrity, reliability, and exceptional service while creating opportunities for others within his community. His mission is not only to provide outstanding cleaning solutions but also to build lasting relationships with clients and establish Debo’s Cleaning Service as a company people can trust for years to come.
